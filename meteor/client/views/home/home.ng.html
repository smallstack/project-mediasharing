About

mediasharing.io is a platform for sharing medias like photos, videos or documents. Users can join sharing groups and televisions or unused LCDs turn into presenters.

Features

Groups

Create a group and invite other users. Nobody outside of your group will see the medias you shared!

Presenters

Use any device that can display a webpage as presenter, for example an Android HDMI Stick plugged into an old LCD, or just a laptop nobody uses anymore. The hardware requirements are very low!

Medias

You can share photos, videos, websites and even powerpoints!
